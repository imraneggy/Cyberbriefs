# Content Strategy

## Positioning

Recommended page:

```text
CyberBriefsDaily
```

Promise:

```text
Cybersecurity news and threats explained in one visual brief.
```

Audience:

- Founders.
- IT admins.
- Students.
- Employees who need scam awareness.
- Cybersecurity beginners.

## Bio

```text
Cyber threats explained visually.
Daily scam, breach, and security briefs.
Educational only. Verify with official advisories.
```

## Content Pillars

- Breach explainers.
- Ransomware timelines.
- Scam warnings.
- CVE explainers.
- AI security risks.
- Cloud security mistakes.
- Password, MFA, and passkey education.
- Business email compromise.
- Weekly cyber recap.

## Daily Structure

Morning:

- Breaking or timely cyber risk.
- Explain what happened and why it matters.

Evening:

- Practical defense checklist.
- Scam warning.
- Concept explainer.

## Caption Template

```text
Hook.

What happened:
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3

Why it matters:
Short explanation.

What to do:
- Action 1
- Action 2
- Action 3

Educational content only. Verify critical security decisions with official advisories.

#CyberSecurity #InfoSec #ScamAlert #Privacy #TechNews
```

## Growth Rules

- Use consistent templates.
- Make every post useful without reading the caption.
- Prefer practical checklists and timelines.
- Keep source quality high.
- Never publish unverified breach claims as fact.
- Track saves, shares, reach, follows, and profile visits.
