# Monetization Guide

## First 90 Days

Do not optimize for ads first. Optimize for saves, shares, and trust.

Daily targets:

- 2 posts per day.
- 1 practical takeaway per post.
- 1 clear visual concept per image.
- 8-15 relevant hashtags.
- No unverified breach claims.

## 0-1k Followers

Focus:

- Consistent design language.
- Clear bio.
- Strong profile promise.
- Save-worthy explainers.

Actions:

- Publish scam checklists.
- Publish ransomware timelines.
- Publish password and MFA explainers.
- Comment on cybersecurity creator posts from the account.

## 1k-5k Followers

Monetization options:

- Affiliate links for password managers.
- Affiliate links for cyber labs and courses.
- PDF security checklists.
- Cybersecurity awareness poster packs.

Avoid heavy sponsored content at this stage.

## 5k-20k Followers

Monetization options:

- Sponsored posts.
- Newsletter sponsorships.
- Paid awareness templates.
- Lead magnets for consulting.

Recommended product:

```text
Small Business Cyber Safety Pack
- Phishing checklist
- Password policy template
- MFA rollout checklist
- Incident response first steps
```

## 20k+ Followers

Monetization options:

- Paid newsletter.
- B2B security awareness content packages.
- Consulting leads.
- Training workshops.
- Sponsored weekly cyber recap.

## Metrics To Track

- Saves per post.
- Shares per post.
- Follows per reach.
- Profile visits.
- Link clicks.
- DM inquiries.
- Sponsored content response rate.

## Monetization Rule

If a monetization offer reduces trust, reject it. Cybersecurity pages grow when the audience believes the page is useful and careful with facts.
